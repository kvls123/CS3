Spy X Family

After completing a mission, Twilight was tasked with a new mission, his new target, Denovan Desmond. However, this is not just one of the many missions he has encountered. There is a catch. He has to get married and have a child in order for him to infiltrate an elite school where Denovan Desmond is always present in social gatherings. Just like its title, it revolves around a family. A very unusual family that consists of a spy, an assassin, and a child that can read minds.
