About Us

The word Capter is a combination of the words Captured and Remember. It is an archive, or a gallery centered website which, of course, showcases photos of VIEWS and SCENERIES or basically, any photo to be remembered. The website allows posting of pictures the author wants to keep for remembrance. That is the goal of this website. Thus, having a tagline of “Capture to Remember!”
